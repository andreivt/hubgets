Your team is the reason why we hurry to work every morning  

 

We believe that people who work to fulfill their
dreams are building the future

We help people communicate and grow together.

We are a small team of dreamers - engineers, designers, and business people. We started this journey five years ago because we wanted a better way to communicate and collaborate at work. And so we did it our way: we built an instant communication software that creates a better work environment. We've been using it ever since, and in 2015 we started to share it with everybody else.

We focus on people because they make the team.

It's not an easy task. We fight barriers that keep us apart: distance, noise, Big Brother. By converging communication flows into a single stream we help team members connect better and build the future we all want.

It's more than software, it's a communication framework.

Fulfilling the craziest dreams takes a lot of effort. We're striving to build an environment that makes work more efficient and pleasant. Everyone, from management to interns, has a unified and consistent view on purpose, goals and progress. Read more about this.
